This is a friendly reminder to enable your Minecraft whitelist.

Enable your whitelist

Your server is publicly reachable

We randomly join discovered Minecraft servers to warn owners that their whitelist is disabled. If this website was send to you, your server is likely open to anyone.

An open whitelist can lead to:

  • Anyone joining without permission
  • Increased risk of griefing and abuse
  • Plugins and admin tools being exposed to strangers
  • Bots, scanners and automated attacks connecting freely
No one likes a giant crater in their world.
Protect your server. Enable your whitelist.

How to enable your whitelist

Most servers can be secured in a few simple steps:

  1. 1. Run: /whitelist on
  2. 2. Add yourself: /whitelist add <username>
  3. 3. Add your trusted players the same way
  4. 4. Optional: restart your server
  5. 5. Done — only whitelisted players can join
